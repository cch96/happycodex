# Optional Fable review

Fable supplements the required native review.

## Select model and availability

Resolve model and effort independently. For each, use this order: explicit current
user instruction, applicable scoped `AGENTS.md`, then the defaults (`Fable 5` and
`max`). Do not silently downgrade, substitute, install, enable, or search for an
external reviewer.

If the authorizing instruction makes Fable conditional, such as “when available,” an
unavailable exact selection continues with native review and reports the skip once;
otherwise stop the task and report.

## Keep reviews independent

Freeze the candidate once. Give native and Fable the same frozen candidate and staged
neutral review brief defined in `native-review.md`. Run them concurrently when
infrastructure allows, but keep both read-only and isolated: each builds its
phase-one inventory independently, and neither receives the writer's defense,
self-review, or the other reviewer's output.

Collect the union of findings; do not vote. The Root independently reproduces or
otherwise verifies every finding before editing.

## Repair and close

Only an explicit user instruction or applicable scoped `AGENTS.md` rule can make
Fable required for completion. This is the sole rerun exception: after a confirmed
repair and the main Skill's checks and fresh native re-review, repeat Fable at most
once. If that rerun has a confirmed or unresolved material finding, stop and report.
